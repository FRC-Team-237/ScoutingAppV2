Help

Autonomous Period

The first phase of each match is 15 seconds long and called the Autonomous Period (auto). During auto, robots operate without any drive team control or input.

Teleoperated Period

The second phase of each match is the remaining 2:15 and called the Teleoperated Period. During this phase, drivers remotely operate robots to retrieve and score game pieces.

Endgame

The final 30 seconds of the teleop stage is the endgame, during which robots attempt to park, dock on, and/or engage with their alliance's charge station or continue to score game pieces.

Mobility

The award given to a robot whose bumpers have completely left its community at any point during auto.

Charge Status

A robot earns points for its alliance by docking on or engaging with their charge station.

A robot is docked if it is contacting only the charge station and/or other items also directly or transitively fully supported by the charge station.

A robot is engaged if the charge station is level and all alliance robots contacting the charge station are docked.
